You'll often see it written as 沢山.

**Using with nouns:**

*   When **たくさん** precedes a noun, it can be directly attached, but using the particle **の** can add emphasis to the noun itself.
    *   「あの店の前に**たくさん**人が集まっている。」 (Focus on the action/situation)
    *   「あの店の前に**沢山**の人が集まっている。」 (Focus on the people)
